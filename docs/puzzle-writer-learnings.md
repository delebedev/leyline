# Puzzle Writer Learnings

Self-reported friction points from the write-puzzle agent. Each entry captures what was hard, what was surprising, and what should change. When the same issue appears 3+ times, it graduates into a skill rule or card palette entry.

### 2026-03-24 — dfc-transform-activate.pzl

**Hardest part:** The transform trigger on Revealing Eye (reveal/choose/discard) creates a second potential hang point beyond the transform itself. Had to think through whether to suppress the trigger or accept dual TIMEOUT modes and document the disambiguation.
**Surprising:** Defender keyword on the front face is a perfect forcing function — the card literally cannot win without transforming. Didn't need any "close off combat" tricks with enemy blockers.
**Would change:** Nothing — DFCs with defender front faces are ideal puzzle subjects. Could become a palette pattern for testing transform/flip mechanics.
